Add Your Voice

Show officials that residents are paying attention.

Add your name in support of a new SR 28 corridor study. It takes thirty seconds — and when CART sits down with ODOT and local leaders, the number of residents behind us is one of the most persuasive things we can bring.

Why it matters

Numbers change the conversation.

  • 1

    Officials count constituents

    A study request backed by hundreds of residents is far harder to set aside than one group's letter.

  • 2

    Your area carries weight

    Telling us where you are helps us show support is coming from across the affected corridor and county.

  • 3

    You set how involved you are

    Adding your name is enough on its own. If you're open to doing more, you can tell us — no pressure.
